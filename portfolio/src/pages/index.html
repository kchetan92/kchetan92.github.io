I am a masters’ student at the University of Michigan studying UX Research and Design. I like to develop people centric design and I enjoy investigating the needs of people and the find best ways to solve them.

I have worked with people from different backgrounds as a graphic designer, web developer and as a product manager on products to make people’s lives better. At the School of Information, University of Michigan, I have taken courses and worked on projects to Investigate users’ needs, behavior and attitude towards a product / service and the best way to solve it.
